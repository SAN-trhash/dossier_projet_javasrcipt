GALLERIE DE CARTES ECHANGES

"I create my works whenever I feel the urge. I don't follow any particular artistic process mechanically. My professor, Kruger Jager, helped me a lot in shaping my production method, whether on canvas or digitally. My art is inspired by the traumas I endured during the trench warfare between the Eldians and the Marleyans. The hope that one day we would all be free from this burden and these walls led me to join a clandestine club where my comrades, survivors of the war, would carve stylish symbols on their chests with knives, and our leader wore a strange old sage costume while sitting at a table with dice. The many war crimes we committed are the primary source of inspiration for my work. And to be honest, I love Dungeons and Dragons."
